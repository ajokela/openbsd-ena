Marker (printed by the ramdisk harness after scraping netstat):
  ENA_P1: rx <n>     # <n> nonzero

The RX path that produces those packets:
  - ena_init() creates the IO RX queue (CREATE_CQ then CREATE_SQ, RX
    direction) and posts mbuf clusters via ena_rx_fill().
  - The device DMA-writes received frames into the clusters and posts
    completions on the RX CQ, raising the IO MSI-X vector (vector 1).
  - ena_intr_io() -> ena_rx_intr() -> ena_rxeof() drains the CQ, hands the
    frames to the stack via if_input(), recycles the slots and refills.
